The Virtual Workplace

Introduction

While some people feel that the most exciting applications for VR technology are in the gaming industry, some however believe it is in fact in business that we will find the most exciting aspects of VR technology.1 The world of business is all about what helps to create a competitive advantage for your company. VR technology may be used in several aspects of the business to help produce this competitive advantage.

VR can help with employee training, company meetings, and working from home. That is only the beginning as it could transform marketing done by companies and even how people buy and browse goods. It is known that many VR systems today are quite expensive for normal consumers to buy, but businesses have the money to invest into the systems. Though the question rises is it good for VR to enter the workplace?

Conclusion

VR technology today is expensive for the average consumer, but a good investment for a company. It has many diverse uses through different workplaces. One view finds this as a great improvement as VR technology can be used to improve training, to help anxiety, or create a working environment where people can meet from across the world. Other people might respond that the VR technology is not going to be good as it will allow business owners to abuse their employees more.

We are approaching an evolution of the business world, whether good or bad. It has yet to been determined the real effect VR will have as the technology is still in its early stages of development. The technology might get cheaper and head to more consumer user or may get more expensive and become exclusively for businesses. It could become a system to design and sell products or it may be a training tool for military units.
